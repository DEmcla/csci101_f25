Understanding different bases: binary, octal, decimal, hexadecimal - essential for computer architecture and programming.

    Development from Babylonian base-60 to modern decimal (Hindu-Arabic) system
  • History of Binary HISTORICAL (1703-present)
    Gottfried Leibniz formalized binary in 1703, George Boole connected it to logic in 1854, foundation of digital computing
  • Boolean Algebra SEMINAL (1854)
    George Boole's algebraic system for logic - basis for binary computing and digital circuits
  • Decimal Number System TUTORIAL
    Place value, powers of 10, the standard number system used in everyday life
  • Hindu-Arabic Numeral System HISTORICAL (~500 CE)
    Origin of modern decimal notation with place value and zero
